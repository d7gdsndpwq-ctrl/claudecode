# Business Name Options

Names are judged on tone (credible, governance-led, not cutesy) and a gut read on domain
availability — not a live registry check. Confirm actual availability before committing (see
SETUP-CHECKLIST.md).

General pattern: plain English word (often custodianship/trust-themed) + "AI" +
Advisory/Governance/Partners. Avoids puns, avoids "Tech-bro" words (Nexus, Synergy, Quantum),
avoids anything that could be confused with an AI product vendor.

| # | Name | Domain read | Notes |
|---|------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | **Custodian AI Advisory** | `.com.au` likely free; `.com` probably taken or parked | ★ Favourite. "Custodian" is the most direct one-word summary of what you actually do with their data. |
| 2 | **Governed AI Partners** | `.com.au` likely free; `.com` contested | Says exactly what it is. Slightly literal/dry — that may be a feature for this audience. |
| 3 | **Verity AI Governance** | `.com.au` likely free; `.com` ("verity.com") long taken, but full phrase probably free | ★ Favourite. "Verity" (truth/accuracy) reads as trust without repeating "governance" twice. |
| 4 | **Steward AI Partners** | `.com.au` likely free; `.com` contested | Same custodianship angle as #1, slightly softer / more "partner" framing. |
| 5 | **Hills AI Governance** | `.com.au` very likely free (geographic + niche) | Most literal regional positioning. Strong if you want the Hills District link obvious from the name itself. |
| 6 | **Northbank AI Advisory** | `.com.au` likely free | Neutral, professional-services-agency feel. No real geographic meaning, just a credible "firm" name. |
| 7 | **Bridgepoint AI Governance** | `.com.au` likely free; `.com` contested (Bridgepoint is used by a UK PE firm) | Solid, slightly generic — check trademark conflict with the existing "Bridgepoint" brand before using. |
| 8 | **Assured AI Partners** | `.com.au` likely free | "Assured" leans into the audit/assurance language accountants already use. |
| 9 | **Trustline AI Advisory** | `.com.au` likely free; `.com` contested (telecom/finance products use "Trustline") | Good fit but check for conflicting use in financial services before relying on it. |
| 10 | **Meridian AI Governance** | `.com.au` likely free; `.com` heavily used across industries | Calm, established-sounding. "Meridian" is a popular name generally, so do a thorough clash check. |

## Favourites

1. **Custodian AI Advisory** — best one-word match to the actual service (you are the custodian
   of how they handle data through AI adoption).
2. **Verity AI Governance** — trust-coded without sounding like a compliance department.
3. **Hills AI Governance** — if you want the local/regional angle to do the positioning work for
   you (useful for local SEO and word-of-mouth referrals in the Hills District).

## Before you commit to one

- Search the exact name (and close variants) on the [ASIC business name register](https://connectonline.asic.gov.au) — this is also where you'll register it.
- Search [IP Australia's trademark register](https://search.ipaustralia.gov.au/trademarks/search/quick) for conflicts, particularly with #7 and #9 above.
- Check the domain on an `.com.au`-accredited registrar (VentraIP, Crazy Domains, GoDaddy.com.au) — `.com.au` registration requires an Australian ABN or registered business name, which is normal and works in your favour once you have one.
